Advisory Board

SDG AI Lab Advisory Board is established as a platform to bring together distinguished professionals around the world with academic, business, civil society, or public experience who share the passion and commitment to use frontier technologies for the achievement of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The Board supports SDG AI Lab and the UN Volunteer Developer Community through strategy guidance, partnerships recommendations and by sharing their invaluable professional
expertise and experiences.
